Target audience: Tech-savvy restaurant owners, chain managers, and upscale hospitality businesses looking for a modern, professional tool.

  • Premium, trustworthy feel — dark themes signal "serious software"
  • Teal accent is energetic yet professional
  • Clean data visualization with strong information hierarchy
  • Easy on eyes during long management sessions
  • Differentiates from typical "friendly" hospitality tools
