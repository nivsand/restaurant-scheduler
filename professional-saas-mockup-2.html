הערות עיצוב

Option 2: Warm Hospitality — Cream + Orange + Brown

Target audience: Small family restaurants, neighborhood cafés, local bakeries, traditional eateries — places where warmth and personal touch matter most.

Strengths:

  • Warm, inviting palette that feels like hospitality — not software
  • Non-intimidating for non-technical restaurant owners
  • Cream/orange/brown evoke food, warmth, and comfort
  • Light theme is easier to read in bright kitchen/restaurant environments
  • Feels personal and approachable

Weaknesses:

  • May feel less "serious" or "enterprise" to larger chains
  • Warm browns can feel dated if not executed carefully
  • Lower price perception compared to dark premium themes
  • Cream backgrounds can be harder to maintain visual contrast

Best for: SMB restaurants in Israel/Mediterranean markets. The aesthetic aligns with food culture, making it an easy sell to non-technical owners who want something friendly and straightforward.
